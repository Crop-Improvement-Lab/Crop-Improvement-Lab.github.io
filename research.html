Our Research

We focus on understanding the molecular mechanisms of plant defense, gene functionality, and applying bioinformatics tools to aid rice breeding. Explore some of our key research areas below.

Rice Disease Resistance

We study bacterial blight, sheath blight to uncover defense mechanisms in rice and help develop resistant varieties.

Gene Functionality

We use advanced functional genomics to identify and characterize genes involved in crucial traits like disease resistance, stress tolerance, and yield enhancement.

NGS and Bioinformatics

Leveraging next-generation sequencing technologies and bioinformatics, we identify novel genetic markers for breeding improved rice varieties.

Abiotic Stress Resistance

Our research focuses on enhancing rice tolerance to abiotic stresses such as drought, salinity, and heat to improve crop yield under adverse conditions.
